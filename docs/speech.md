### Introduction: The Hook (Story Time)
**Visual:** A photo of Dhanushkodi/Ram Setu or just a blank screen with the speaker in focus.
**Speaker:**
"Good morning everyone. Before I begin, I want to share a quick story.

Recently, my brother and I traveled to **Dhanushkodi**, about 23 kilometers from Rameswaram, to visit the historic **Ram Setu**. It was beautiful. We met local merchants there selling incredible handmade wooden crafts and ornaments made from sea treasures.

I wanted to buy a lot of things to support them. But there was a problem.
I wasn't carrying cash. The nearest ATM was 23 kilometers away. My phone had absolutely **no network signal**, and these humble merchants didn't have QR codes.

I felt helpless. I had exactly 20 rupees in my pocket, with which I bought this single bracelet **[Show Bracelet to audience]**.

As I stood there, I thought: **'What if we had an offline digital payment system that didn't need a signal, didn't need a server, and didn't need a QR code?'**"

**Visual:** A split screen. Left: Someone struggling with a QR code in a crowd. Right: A 'Payment Failed' server error screen.
**Speaker:**
"India is the global leader in digital payments via UPI. But my story at Dhanushkodi highlights three massive cracks in the system:

2.  **Friction:** To pay ₹20 for tea, I have to pull out my phone, unlock it, scan a code, and enter a PIN. It’s slow.
3.  **The Environment:** We spend trillions digitally, but how does that help the climate? 'Green Banking' is currently just a corporate buzzword, not a consumer reality."

---

### Slide 4: Key Technology (How it Works)
**Visual:** Diagram showing: Phone (Online) -> Watch (Offline Ledger) -> Merchant (BLE).
**Speaker:**
"So, how does it work? We utilize a **Distributed State Machine** architecture.
1.  **Load:** You load funds—say ₹500—from your phone to the watch while online.
2.  **Spend:** The watch stores this value in a secure, encrypted local ledger. When you pay, it talks directly to the merchant terminal via Bluetooth or NFC. **No internet required.**
3.  **Sync:** Once you are back online, the watch syncs with the phone, updating the bank servers."

*Pause for effect.*
"It’s like carrying digital cash, but smarter."
